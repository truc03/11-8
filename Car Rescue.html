24/7 car rescue service

BUTL’s 24/7 car rescue service provides fast and reliable support solutions, helping you safely resolve any emergency situation on the road, regardless of time and location such as: car breakdown on the road, accidents, or unexpected technical problems, etc.

Use the service now

How to use 24/7 car rescue service at BUTL

→
Contact for rescue request

In case of an accident, you can open the BUTL app and select the 24/7 Auto Rescue service. BUTL will record the accident information and your location.

→
Waiting for BUTL rescue team

Once the information is received, a rescue team will be dispatched to your location as soon as possible. You will be updated on the progress and estimated time of arrival of the rescue team.

→
Troubleshooting

BUTL’s team of technicians will assess the condition of your vehicle and fix the problem on the spot if possible. If necessary, your vehicle will be towed to the nearest garage or your requested location for repairs.

→
Pay

After using the service, you can pay the service fee directly on the BUTL application, the cost will be displayed clearly and transparently.
