Introduce

24/7 Motorcycle Rescue Service

BUTL’s 24/7 motorbike rescue service was created to provide quick and effective support, helping you overcome any emergency situation such as: running out of gas, flat tire, engine failure or even minor accidents, especially on deserted roads or when you travel at night. Wherever you are, with just a few operations on the BUTL application, the rescue team will come to support and help you continue your journey safely.

Use the service now

How to use 24/7 motorbike rescue service at BUTL

→
Contact for rescue request

In case of an accident, you can contact the rescue service via the BUTL app, providing information about the accident and your location so that the BUTL rescue team can reach you as quickly as possible.

→
Waiting for BUTL rescue team

Once we receive the information, BUTL will confirm your request and dispatch the nearest rescue team to your location. You will receive an update on the estimated time of arrival of the rescue team.

→
Troubleshooting

The rescue team will check and fix simple technical problems. In more complicated cases, your car will be taken to the nearest garage or the place you request for repair.

→
Pay

After using the service, you can pay the service fee directly on the BUTL application, the cost will be displayed clearly and transparently.
