Introduce

Parking lot connection sevice

BUTL's parking lot connection service helps you easily find and book safe parking spaces through the application without having to spend time and effort searching for parking lots in the city, and also connects with reputable parking lots in many different locations to create the most convenience for customers.Taking your car for regular inspections is often time-consuming and laborious, especially when you are busy or unfamiliar with the complex process. Therefore, BUTL provides inspection services, helping customers save time and eliminate all worries about administrative procedures. Vehicle inspection is not difficult, BUTL takes care of it!

How to use the Parking Lot Link service at BUTL

→

Find parking

Use the parking lot linking feature on the BUTL app to find the nearest linked parking lots. Choose the right parking lot based on location and cost that suits you..

→

Make a reservation

Once you have selected a parking space, you can make a reservation to ensure that there will be a parking space available when you arrive.

→

Safe parking

When you arrive at the parking lot, you just need to present your booking information via the app, the parking lot staff will guide you to park and ensure the safety of your vehicle during the storage period..

→

Easy Payment

After using the parking service, you can pay the parking fee directly on the application, making payment faster and more convenient, without having to use cash or wait long like before.
