Introduce

Registration forhousehold

Taking your car for regular inspections is often time-consuming and laborious, especially when you are busy or unfamiliar with the complex process. Therefore, BUTL provides inspection services, helping customers save time and eliminate all worries about administrative procedures. Vehicle inspection is not difficult, BUTL takes care of it!

Vehicle inspection process at BUTL

→

Book service via BUTL app

You schedule an inspection via the BUTL app by providing vehicle information and desired time.

→

Pick up at your door

BUTL team will come to pick up the vehicle and conduct inspection, process all necessary documents before taking the vehicle to the inspection center.

→

Vehicle inspection

Your vehicle will be taken to a reputable inspection center, BUTL will conduct the entire inspection process, ensuring the vehicle meets the standards required by law.

→

Return the car

After the inspection is completed, the vehicle will be returned to you, along with complete inspection certificates.
