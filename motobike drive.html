Introduce

Motorcycle Driver Service

BUTL's motorbike rental service is the perfect solution for those who need to move quickly and safely around the city but cannot or do not want to drive themselves. This service is especially useful when you have consumed alcohol and cannot drive yourself home.

With a team of professional, friendly and knowledgeable motorbike drivers, BUTL helps you get to your destination quickly but still ensures safety. Whether you need to go to work, go to school, attend an important appointment, or simply go home after a fun party, BUTL is always ready to serve you.

Book a Motorbike driver by:

1 Step 1: Enter Pickup Point/Destination
2 Step 2: Select service Car Driver/Motorbike Driver/Provincial Driver
3 Step 3: Select payment method
4 Step 4: Enter promo code (if any)
5 Step 5: Select Book a ride and let BUTL find the nearest driver for you

Commitment to service quality

Community mission: With the motto “Your Safety Is Our Happiness”, BUTL has purchased a professional liability insurance package specifically for BUTL’s services.

Driver team: With a team of experienced drivers who are carefully selected and trained, BUTL always wants vehicle owners to be comfortable, safe and relaxed on every trip.

No worries about price: The price of the trip is displayed right on the BUTL app (including promotional/discount prices). Just pay the exact amount and enjoy the trip.
